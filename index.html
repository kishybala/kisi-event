Lead like a Girl

13 September 2024

Welcome

"Lead Like a Girl" is an initiative dedicated to empowering women, particularly in male-dominated fields such as technology, to embrace leadership roles with confidence and authenticity. This concept challenges traditional gender norms, encouraging women to lead in ways that align with their strengths, perspectives, and values, rather than conforming to stereotypical leadership styles.
